## CSM Usage Notes

### High-Value Products for Upselling
1. **Event-based Add-ons** (highest revenue potential):
   - Identified Events
   - Group Analytics (B2B companies)
   - Data Pipelines

2. **Revenue Analytics** - Critical for tracking business outcomes
3. **Session Replay** - High adoption rate, clear ROI
4. **Feature Flags + Experiments** - Engineering teams love these
5. **Data Warehouse** - Enterprise feature for data-heavy orgs

### Product Adoption Indicators
- **Trivial/Experimental:** <$100 monthly spend
- **Adopted:** $100+ monthly spend  
- **Significant:** $500+ monthly spend

### Integration Complexity
- **Easy:** Product Analytics, Web Analytics, Feature Flags
- **Medium:** Session Replay, Surveys, Experiments
- **Complex:** Data Warehouse, LLM Observability, Revenue Analytics
